Intro

Greetings! I'm an enthusiastic second-year Computer Science student at the University of Windsor, driven by an unwavering passion for all things related to computers and technology. Hailing from a Bengali background, I bring a rich cultural perspective to my work, embracing diversity and fostering meaningful connections with people from all walks of life.

Ever since my earliest memories, I've been captivated by the world of computers and the endless possibilities they offer. My journey has seen me spend countless hours exploring the virtual realms of the internet, refining my technical skills, and nurturing my innate curiosity. I thrive on challenges and actively seek opportunities to step out of my comfort zone, as I believe growth happens beyond the familiar. My natural charisma and leadership qualities have allowed me to excel in team settings. I thoroughly enjoy collaborating with diverse groups of individuals, combining our strengths to achieve common goals. Whether it's leading a coding project, participating in hackathons, or engaging in community-building initiatives, I'm energized by the power of collaboration. Beyond the lines of code and technical complexities, my heart lies in connecting with people. My ability to communicate effectively and empathetically is an asset that I continually hone. I find joy in mentoring and guiding my peers, helping them grasp intricate concepts and fostering an environment of collective learning.
